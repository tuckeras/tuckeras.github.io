Don't Eat Me

Team size: 10

Team Makeup
  • Two Designers
  • Two Programmers
  • Five Artists

Project Description

Don't Eat Me is a story game modeled after a traditional storybook. The game follows the story of a child named Pico who has to navigate encounters with different monsters meant to represent the traditional fears of growing up.

This game was created for the cornerstone project for my degree. Don't Eat Me development started in September of 2023, with Development finishing in April of 2024. My contributions to the project were largely in the producer role, with me managing distribution of tasks and working as an intermediary between the different departments. Due to the small team size, I did work as a member on the design team, and also was responsible for the design and implentation of the user interface.
